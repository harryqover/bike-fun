Collaborating on Web Apps: A Simple Guide

A simple and effective process for building and improving web applications connected to Google Sheets.

  • 1
    Share Your Code: Provide the complete index.html and Code.gs files. This gives a full picture of the project.
  • 2
    State Your Goal: Clearly explain the bug, the desired improvement, or the new feature. The more specific, the better!
  • 3
    Get Complete Code Back: You'll receive fully updated, commented code with a version number logged to the console for easy verification. No snippets, just the complete, working files.
