Welcome to the ASCII Ant Farm!

        
This is a small, interactive simulation of an ant colony's life. You can watch as the ants work together to find food, grow their colony, and avoid dangers.

        
        

            

                
The Cast of Characters

                
    
                    
  • & Ant: A worker ant searching for food.
    • 
                    
  • @ Ant with Food: Carrying food back to the nest.
    • 
                    
  • Q The Queen: Stays in the nest and lays eggs.
    • 
                    
  • o Egg: Will hatch into a new ant.
    • 
                    
  • █ The Anthill: The center of the colony.
    • 
                    
  • * Food: The resource your ants need to collect.
    • 
                    
  • S Spider: A dangerous predator!
    • 
                    
  • # Obstacle: A solid wall or rock.
    • 
                    
  • ·:∴ Pheromones: Scent trails ants use to find paths.

How It Works

                
    
                    
  1. Foraging: Ants wander randomly until they find food.
    2. 
                    
  2. Pheromone Trails: Ants leave scent trails to guide other ants to food, creating efficient supply lines.
    3. 
                    
  3. Colony Growth: The Queen lays eggs that hatch into new workers, growing the colony automatically.
    4. 
                    
  4. Survival: Spiders wander the map and will eat any ants they come across.
