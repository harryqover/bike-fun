Settings

How to get your Script URL:

  1. Create a new Google Sheet for your library.
  2. In the sheet, go to Extensions > Apps Script.
  3. Paste the provided Code.gs content and save the project.
  4. Click the blue Deploy button, then New deployment.
  5. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. This is important!
  6. Click Deploy and authorize the script when prompted.
  7. Copy the final Web app URL and paste it into the field above.
