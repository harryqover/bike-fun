Welcome to the ASCII Bee Farm!

        
Watch your bees work together to find flowers, produce honey, and protect their Queen from hornets.

        
        

            

                
The Cast of Characters

                
    
                    
  • B Bee: A worker bee searching for nectar.
    • 
                    
  • 8 Bee with Nectar: Carrying nectar to the hive.
    • 
                    
  • Q The Queen Bee: Stays in the hive and lays eggs.
    • 
                    
  • o Egg: Will hatch into a new bee.
    • 
                    
  • # The Beehive: Center of the colony and honey storage.
    • 
                    
  • * Flower: Source of nectar for your bees.
    • 
                    
  • H Hornet: A dangerous predator!
    • 
                    
  • ·:∴ Scent Trails: Faint trails bees use to find paths.
    • 
                

            

            

                
How It Works

                
    
                    
  1. Foraging: Bees wander to find flowers (`*`) and collect nectar.
    2. 
                    
  2. Honey Production: When a bee (`8`) returns to the hive (`#`), it adds 1 unit of honey.
    3. 
                    
  3. Honey Collection: Click the "Collect Honey" button to add the hive's honey to your total score!
    4. 
                    
  4. Colony Growth: The Queen (`Q`) lays eggs that hatch into new workers.
    5. 
                    
  5. Survival: Hornets (`H`) will hunt your bees. Be careful!
    6.
